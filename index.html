FAQ

Why is the cost of this program relatively lower?

Our goal is to identify and nurture talented interns who can contribute to our internal projects. We believe that financial constraints should never hinder deserving and capable individuals from joining the program.

How is the IRP Program different from traditional programs?

The IRP Program is designed to provide practical, hands-on training that enables interns to deliver effective and high-quality work during their project internships, focusing on real-world delivery guided by experts.

Is this zhapix teaching institute or an IT Company?

Zhapix Technologies is an IT company (OPC) Private Limited. We provide expert services and are building a product in the Digital Interaction Domain. Our Center of Excellence trains internal teams and aspiring IT professionals to support this vision.

Can I skip the Bootcamp if I already know the MERN stack?

Sure.You can send an email to irp.contact@zhapix.com expressing your interest. You will then go through an interview process, and upon successful evaluation, you will be onboarded as an intern.

Does the IRP Program guarantee a stipend or job placement?

The IRP Program is a skill-building initiative designed to develop industry-standard abilities through expert training and practical tools. It is not a job-seeking program, but high-performing interns may receive a stipend or be considered for future job opportunities based on KPIs and availability.
